In the sme_scaledMicrobialBiomass and sme_microbialBiomass tables, sample-level results in which particular lipids are out of the range of instrument calibration are reported in the field analysisResultsQF; batch-level issues with QA standards or analytical blanks are reported in the field analyteStandardQF. Other known issues may be reported in the dataQF field for affected records.
